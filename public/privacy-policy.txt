PRIVACY POLICY
Mission101.ai – Meta App & LinkedIn App

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mission101 AI ("Mission101.ai," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with (1) our Meta App that publishes content to the mission101.ai Instagram account (@mission101.ai), and (2) our LinkedIn App that uses the Community Management API to manage our LinkedIn presence. This policy applies to users who interact with our Meta App, our LinkedIn App, and to visitors of our website at https://mission101.ai.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. INTRODUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mission101.ai operates a Meta App that integrates with Instagram to publish content to our official Instagram account (mission101.ai), and a LinkedIn App that uses LinkedIn's Community Management API to manage our LinkedIn presence. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 Information Collected Through the Meta App

When you use or interact with our Meta App that publishes to the mission101.ai Instagram account, we may collect:

- Account and profile information: Instagram account identifiers, profile information, and content you authorize us to access through Meta's APIs
- Content data: Images, captions, and other media used for publishing to our Instagram account
- Usage information: How you interact with our app, including publishing activity and engagement metrics
- Technical data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and server logs

2.2 Information Obtained Through Meta Business Tools

We use Meta Business Tools (including the Instagram API) to manage and publish content. When you connect or authorize our app, Meta may share with us:

- Data necessary for content publishing and account management
- Analytics and insights related to published content
- Information required to provide and improve our services

2.3 Information Collected Through the LinkedIn App (Community Management API)

When you use or interact with our LinkedIn App that uses the Community Management API, we may collect:

- Page and profile data: LinkedIn Page data, administrator data, organization profile information, and content you authorize us to access through LinkedIn's APIs
- Member analytics data: Engagement on posts (likes, comments, reactions), follower statistics, and content performance metrics
- Page messaging data: Messages and related Member Profile data associated with the authors of messages (for Page Messaging only)
- Events data: Organization events and related metadata

We comply with LinkedIn's Data Storage Requirements: member social activity data (e.g., posts, comments, likes) is stored for no more than 48 hours, and most member profile data is stored for no more than 24 hours. Member data is used only to manage our LinkedIn Page and Profile; we do not use it for advertising, sales, recruiting, lead creation, or ad targeting.

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website or use our services:

- Browser and device information (e.g., browser type, operating system)
- Server and usage information (e.g., pages visited, referring URLs)
- Usage patterns and interaction data

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use the information we collect to:

- Publish and manage content on the mission101.ai Instagram account
- Manage our LinkedIn presence via the Community Management API (Page Management, Member Profile Management, Page Messaging, Events Management, Member Post Analytics)
- Provide, operate, and maintain our Meta App, LinkedIn App, and related services
- Recognize you when you return to our app or website
- Improve our services, user experience, and content quality
- Analyze usage patterns and measure the effectiveness of our content
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
- Send promotional communications (where you have consented)
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 Sharing with Meta/Instagram

We share data with Meta (Facebook, Inc.) and Instagram as necessary to operate our Meta App, publish content, and use Meta Business Tools. Meta's use of data is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy (https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/).

4.2 Sharing with LinkedIn

We share data with LinkedIn (Microsoft Corporation) as necessary to operate our LinkedIn App and use the Community Management API. LinkedIn's use of data is governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy (https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy). We do not export, distribute, or transfer LinkedIn member data outside our application, and we do not combine LinkedIn member data with other data sources to create or supplement user profiles or leads.

4.3 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with vendors and service providers who assist us in operating our app and website, including:

- Hosting and infrastructure providers
- Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics)
- Customer support tools

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, and secure hosting. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

In compliance with LinkedIn's Data Storage Requirements, we store LinkedIn member social activity data for no more than 48 hours and most LinkedIn member profile data for no more than 24 hours. Data received through the Community Management API is used only to manage our LinkedIn Page and Profile and is not retained beyond these limits.
